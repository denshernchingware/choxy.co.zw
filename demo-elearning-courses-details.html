Heritage Based Curriculum

At Choxy Dee Academy, we implement the Heritage Based Curriculum (HBC), which integrates traditional Zimbabwean values and knowledge with modern educational standards. This approach ensures our students develop a strong sense of identity while gaining globally competitive skills.

Our curriculum covers Forms 1 through 4, preparing students for their Ordinary Level examinations with a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical application.
