SCHOOL HISTORY

A Growing Legacy

CHOXY DEE ACADEMY is a private secondary school located in Glen Norah C, Highglen District. Established in 2022, the school serves Forms 1-4 with a strong academic track record of over 85% pass rate.

In just a few years, we have built a reputation for academic rigor, dedicated teaching staff, and a nurturing environment that allows every student to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that nurtures academic excellence, integrity, innovation, and leadership. We are committed to developing students who are prepared to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To develop well-rounded learners who excel academically and socially in an ever-changing world. We envision graduates who are confident, capable, and committed to lifelong learning.
