School History

A Growing Legacy

CHOXY DEE ACADEMY is a private secondary school located in Glen Norah C, Highglen District. Established in 2022, the school serves Forms 1-4 with a strong academic track record of over 85% pass rate. In just a few years, we have built a reputation for academic rigor, dedicated teaching staff, and a nurturing environment that allows every student to reach their full potential.
